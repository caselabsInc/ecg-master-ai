# ECG-Master Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 23, 2026

## Who we are

ECG-Master is operated by **Viomath Tech**, located in Tati Siding, Botswana, Africa. ECG-Master provides clinician-only educational decision support for structured ECG review. You can contact us at **support@ecgmaster-ai.com**.

## Scope

This policy describes information handled by the ECG-Master mobile app, marketing website, app access workflows, support channels, and related services operated by Viomath Tech.

## Information we collect

- **Account information** — name, email address, and authentication identifiers. ECG-Master supports sign-in with email/password, Google, and Apple.
- **ECG report content** entered by users, including structured observations and measurements.
- **Patient context** entered by users into a report. This can include age, gender, date of birth, clinical indication, and the ECG date/time. Users should avoid entering unnecessary patient identifiers.
- **AI-generated interpretation support** produced from the structured entries.
- **Support information** — the messages you send us, plus your account email, display name, app version, and device model and operating system, which are attached to support requests for troubleshooting.
- **Service and usage records** required to operate the service, including limited request-rate records used to protect the service.

## How we use information

- Authentication and account access.
- Saving structured reports and report history.
- Generating clinician-reviewable educational interpretation support.
- Support, troubleshooting, safety review, abuse prevention, and reliability improvement.

## AI processing

To generate educational support text, structured ECG report data — including the age, gender, and clinical indication you enter — is sent to **Google (the Gemini API / Google Generative Language service)**. AI output is educational only. It is not autonomous diagnosis, treatment direction, emergency guidance, or a replacement for clinician review.

## Data sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us run ECG-Master:

- **Google Firebase and Google Cloud** — hosting, authentication, database storage, app integrity (App Check), and serverless functions.
- **Apple and Google** — authentication when you choose Sign in with Apple or Google.
- **Google (Gemini API)** — to produce educational interpretation support text.
- Support or operations providers where needed to run the service.

We do **not** sell personal data. We do **not** use advertising tracking in ECG-Master.

## Health and clinical data responsibility

Users should not enter unnecessary patient identifiers. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the institutional, privacy, legal, and professional authority required before entering clinical data into ECG-Master.

## Security

Information is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections and stored using Google Cloud / Firebase infrastructure with access controls that restrict authenticated users to their own report data. We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information handled by ECG-Master. No system can be guaranteed completely secure.

## Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, support users, meet operational needs, comply with applicable obligations, and maintain safety or reliability records. When you delete your account, your account and associated report data are removed as described below.

## Your rights, account deletion, and data requests

You can:

- **Delete your account and associated data directly in the app** (Profile → Delete account), which removes your account and report data.
- Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information, or other data assistance, by contacting **support@ecgmaster-ai.com**.

Some information may be retained where required for security, legal, operational, or safety reasons. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, deletion, portability, or objection); contact us to exercise them.

## Children

ECG-Master is intended for licensed clinicians, supervised clinical learners, educators, and trusted reviewers. It is not intended for children or patient-directed use.

## International processing

Viomath Tech is based in Botswana, and information may be processed in other countries depending on our service providers and infrastructure (including Google Cloud regions). Users are responsible for confirming their authority to enter clinical information.

## Contact

For privacy questions, support, account, or data requests, contact **support@ecgmaster-ai.com**.
